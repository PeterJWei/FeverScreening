ICSL Fever Screening

A system for early detection of fever in populations to improve screening.

About

One of the main challenges towards containing infectious diseases such as COVID-19, is the high possibility of infection to others in public spaces. However, many public spaces such as airports, malls and restaurants, as well as work locations are imperative to a functioning economy. To help aid small businesses to help screen potential carriers of infectious diseases, our system can provide early detection of fever remotely, without any physical contact.

System Design

Thermal Sensing

Our system uses a commercial thermal camera for easy deployment at low cost.

People Detection

We automatically detect people to enable placement in different environments.

Robust Models

We develop accurate models to ensure correct identification of sick occupants.

Mobile Alerts

The system syncs with your smartphone, for real-time alerts about sick occupants.

System Details

Figure 1: System Architecture.
Our system is composed of six main subcomponents. The different subcomponents connect to extract features from the RGB and thermal images, and output fever predictions for any occupants in view.
  • YOLO Head Detection
  • Distance Regression
  • RGB Orientation
  • Matching and Tracking
  • Model Mapping
  • Fever Prediction
